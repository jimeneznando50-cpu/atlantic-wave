🌊 Who We Are

About Atlantic Wave

Atlantic Wave is a digital tourism marketplace born from a deep passion for Puerto Plata and a vision to connect the world with the authentic Caribbean spirit that makes this city extraordinary.

We bridge the gap between tourists seeking genuine local experiences and the talented entrepreneurs, restaurateurs, artisans, and tour operators who make Puerto Plata one of the Dominican Republic's most vibrant destinations.

🎯 Our Mission

Empower local businesses to reach global travelers while delivering authentic, unforgettable Caribbean experiences.

🔭 Our Vision

Become the leading digital tourism platform for the entire North Coast of the Dominican Republic.

💙 Authenticity

Every business on our platform is handpicked for quality, hospitality, and genuine local character.

🌱 Community

We invest in local talent and ensure tourism revenue benefits the people of Puerto Plata directly.
